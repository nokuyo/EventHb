About Us

Our Mission

EventHub is dedicated to connecting communities on college campuses by making it easy to create, discover, and attend local events. We believe in fostering engagement through innovative location-based services and a fun, interactive experience for everyone.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, EventHub was born out of a desire to bring people together. Our team, comprising Simon Meili, Zachary Felcyn, and Luis Zarate has a passion for technology and community building. With experience in web development and a drive for innovation, we built EventHub to serve as a catalyst for meaningful campus interactions.

Our Team

Our team is committed to delivering a user-friendly platform that leverages the latest web technologies. We continuously strive to improve the experience by listening to feedback and adopting best practices in design and development.
